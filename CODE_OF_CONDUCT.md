# Community Code of Conduct

Be respectful, technically honest, and safety-conscious.

- Discuss ideas and evidence, not personal characteristics.
- Do not pressure users to run risky commands or flash uncertain firmware.
- Clearly label hypotheses and unverified findings.
- Protect private network details and credentials.
- Accept review and correction professionally.
- Report harmful behavior to the maintainers through a private channel.

Maintainers may remove content or participation that risks users, violates privacy, or disrupts constructive collaboration.
